FAQ
Why is my PC suddenly so slow even though nothing changed?
Background processes, malware, or a full hard drive can cause sudden slowdowns even without visible changes. Try restarting, checking Task Manager, and running a disk cleanup.
How do I get better FPS without upgrading my whole setup?
Lowering in-game settings, updating GPU drivers, closing background apps, and enabling Game Mode in Windows can significantly improve FPS without new hardware.
What parts should I upgrade first on a budget?
RAM and an SSD give the most noticeable performance gains for the price. If gaming, the GPU is usually the next priority after storage.
This error keeps popping up, how do I fix it?
Note the error code or message and search it along with your OS version. Most common errors have documented fixes on Microsoft's support pages or community forums.
Any tips to make my setup cleaner or more efficient?
Cable management, removing unused apps, and organizing your desktop go a long way. Use tools like CCleaner for software clutter and zip ties for cables.
Why do some apps run at startup even when I didn't open them?
Many apps add themselves to the startup list during installation. You can manage these in Task Manager under the Startup tab and disable what you don't need.
What's really happening when my system freezes or crashes?
Freezes and crashes are often caused by overheating, RAM issues, driver conflicts, or failing storage. Check Event Viewer logs and monitor temps with HWMonitor.
Why does my PC get slower the longer it stays on?
Memory leaks from apps and accumulated temporary files can degrade performance over time. Regular restarts and keeping software updated help prevent this.
